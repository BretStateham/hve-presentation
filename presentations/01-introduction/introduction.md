## What is Hypervelocity Engineering?

HVE is a way of working where **expert multidisciplinary teams** apply AI, proven accelerators, and design thinking across the SDLC to **maximize quality and accelerate value**.

> HVE focuses on improving **how software is built**, not just making code faster to write.
